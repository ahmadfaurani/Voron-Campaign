## Database Integrity Notes

### EPF Structural Clarification
EPF (KWSP) operates as a **single statutory body** under Ministry of Finance. Unlike PNB (which has ASNB, PNB Capital, etc. as separate legal entities), EPF's investment functions are managed internally by:
- Investment Division (Public Markets)
- Investment Division (Private Markets)
- Investment Division (Real Estate)
- Investment Division (Fixed Income)

**Recommendation:** For campaign targeting, treat "EPF-Linked Finance 2/3" as **internal divisions** rather than separate companies. Outreach should target KWSP Investment Division heads by asset class.

### Sandbox Graduation Tracking
5 of 10 "Sandbox Fintech" placeholders have graduated to full digital banking licenses (GXBank, Boost, AEON, KAF, Ryt). These should be **reclassified from Tier 6 (Fintech Sandbox) to Tier 2 (Digital Banks)** for campaign prioritization.
