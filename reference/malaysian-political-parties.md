## Coalition Structure

### Pakatan Harapan (PH) - Ruling Coalition
**Status:** Federal Government (as of 2026)  
**Key Parties:**
- **PKR** (Parti Keadilan Rakyat) - Multi-ethnic, reformist
- **DAP** (Democratic Action Party) - Multicultural, social democratic
- **AMANAH** (Parti Amanah Negara) - Progressive Islamist
- **UPKO** (United Progressive Kinabalu Organisation) - Sabah-based

**Leadership:**
- Prime Minister: Anwar Ibrahim (PKR)
- PKR President: Anwar Ibrahim
- DAP Secretary-General: Anthony Loke
- AMANAH President: Mohamad Sabu

---

## Historical Context

### Major Political Events

#### 2018 General Election (GE14)
- PH wins first federal government
- End of BN 61-year rule
- Anwar Ibrahim becomes PM (after Mahathir transition)

#### 2020 Sheraton Move
- PH government collapses
- Muhyiddin Yassin becomes PM (PN-BN coalition)
- PKR splits (Azmin Ali faction leaves)

#### 2022 General Election (GE15)
- Hung parliament
- Anwar Ibrahim becomes PM (unity government)
- BN reduced to kingmaker role

#### 2026 Current Crisis
- PKR Johor facing defections
- BERSAMA emerges as third force
- Monitor for cascade effects

## Monitoring Notes

### Red Flags for Instability
1. Branch chief resignations (PKR Johor - ACTIVE)
2. Closed-door meetings (unusual timing)
3. Media silence from key figures
4. Sudden travel abroad (leadership)
5. Social media activity spikes

### Positive Stability Indicators
1. Public unity statements
2. Joint appearances
3. Coordinated messaging
4. Membership renewal rates
5. Grassroots event attendance
