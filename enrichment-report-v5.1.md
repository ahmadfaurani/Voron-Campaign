### Verified Gaps (Confirmed Not Publicly Available)

**Institutions with inaccessible leadership pages:**
- Tabung Haji: Nuxt.js SPA, management tab content does not render for scrapers
- BSN: Only Board of Directors page exists, no senior management page
- LPPSA: Management team presented as images only (no text data)
- MARA: Management team presented as images only (no text data)

**Foreign banks with no accessible Malaysian leadership pages:**
- HSBC Bank Malaysia, Citibank, BNP Paribas, ICBC, Deutsche Bank, SMBC, J.P. Morgan, Mizuho, Credit Suisse
- These typically have small Malaysian operations with shared regional functions

**CISO role rarely publicly listed:**
- Only Berjaya Sompo had an identifiable info security leader (Senior Manager level, not CISO title)
- Most insurers/banks do not publicly name their CISO

**GRC role not standard in Malaysian insurance:**
- The "Governance, Risk & Compliance" title is not commonly used in Malaysian financial institutions
- These functions are typically split between CRO (risk) and Head of Compliance (compliance)
