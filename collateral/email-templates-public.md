## How to Use These Templates

These email templates are designed for outreach to BNM-regulated financial institutions regarding RMiT compliance. Customize the bracketed fields `[Like This]` with your specific information.

### Best Practices:
1. **Personalize:** Add recipient name, institution name, and specific references
2. **Timing:** Send on Tuesday-Thursday, 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM MYT
3. **Follow-Up:** Use 8-touch cadence over 28 days
4. **Value-First:** Lead with helpful content, not sales pitch
5. **Clear CTA:** One clear call-to-action per email

## Usage Guidelines

### A/B Testing Recommendations:
- **Subject Lines:** Test urgency vs. value-focused
- **CTAs:** Test "30-minute call" vs. "send resources"
- **Timing:** Test Tuesday 10 AM vs. Thursday 2 PM
- **Length:** Test short (100 words) vs. detailed (250 words)

### Personalization Levels:
- **Level 1 (Basic):** First name, institution name
- **Level 2 (Segment):** Tier-specific messaging, peer references
- **Level 3 (Hyper):** Board member names, recent news, mutual connections

### Compliance Notes:
- Ensure all claims are accurate and verifiable
- Don't guarantee compliance outcomes
- Include unsubscribe option in all marketing emails
- Respect PDPA requirements for data handling
