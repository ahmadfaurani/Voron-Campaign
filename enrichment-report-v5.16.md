## Methodology

Three parallel subagents (delegate_task) with web toolsets researched 6 institutions across 2 batches:
- **Subagent A:** J.P. Morgan Chase Bank Malaysia + Deutsche Bank (Malaysia)
- **Subagent B:** AmMetLife Insurance + MSIG Insurance (Malaysia)
- **Subagent C:** Maybank Investment Bank + SMBC Malaysia

Each subagent was instructed to:
1. Check official corporate websites and leadership/management pages
2. Check BNM regulatory filings (Pillar 3 Disclosures, audited financial statements, SORMIC)
3. Check annual reports and corporate governance statements
4. NOT report the CEO (not a target role)
5. Return name, exact title, source URL, confidence (0-100) for each role found
6. Say NOT FOUND with source if not publicly listed — do NOT fabricate

Subagents collectively scraped 80+ official PDF documents (CG statements, audited FS, Pillar 3 disclosures, SORMIC, annual reports, board charters) and ran 12+ firecrawl agent jobs and 40+ web searches.
