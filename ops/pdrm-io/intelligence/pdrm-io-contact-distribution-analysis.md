## 🎯 Standard Article Template (Observed)

All 6 articles follow this structure:

```markdown
## Headline
"Polis cari [NAME] bantu siasatan [CASE TYPE]"

## Lead Paragraph
- Location (city/state)
- Police district seeking assistance
- Subject name and brief description

## Subject Details
- Full name
- Age (if available)
- Last known address
- Case type / legal section

## Contact Information (ALWAYS PRESENT)
- IO name and rank
- IO mobile (83% of cases)
- Office landline (100% of cases)
- Extension (50% of cases)
- Alternative: "nearest police station" (100%)

## Official Quote
- Senior officer statement (Supt./DSP/Comm level)
- Direct quote in Malay or English
- Source attribution

## Call to Action
- "Orang ramai yang mempunyai maklumat..."
- Contact instructions repeated
```

## 📋 Implications for Journalist Registry

### Contact Verification Protocol

**Tier 1 (Highest Confidence):**
- Mobile numbers published in news articles
- Cross-referenced with official PDRM directory office lines
- IO name + rank matches district/unit

**Tier 2 (Medium Confidence):**
- Office lines matching PDRM directory
- Extensions verified against district switchboard
- Multiple sources confirm same IO

**Tier 3 (Pattern-Inferred - DO NOT ACTIVATE):**
- Email patterns (`bp_dcc[district]@rmp.gov.my`)
- Unverified mobile numbers from non-official sources
- Social media DM contacts

### Integration Strategy

1. **News Article Monitoring:**
   - Track "polis cari" + "bantu siasatan" patterns
   - Extract IO contacts from all major outlets
   - Timestamp each extraction

2. **Cross-Reference with PDRM Directory:**
   - Verify office lines against rmp.gov.my
   - Confirm district/unit assignments
   - Flag discrepancies for manual review

3. **Confidence Scoring:**
   - News-published mobile: **HIGH** (direct from PDRM statement)
   - News-published office: **HIGH** (verified against directory)
   - Pattern-inferred email: **LOW** (do not activate)

4. **Update Cadence:**
   - Real-time: News article extraction
   - Weekly: PDRM directory cross-check
   - Monthly: Confidence score review

## 🎯 Recommendations

### For Media Relations Tracking
1. **Prioritize mobile numbers** from news articles - these are IO-approved for public contact
2. **Track IO promotions** - rank changes indicate career progression (DSP → ACP → Supt.)
3. **Map IO beats** - which journalists cover which districts/units regularly
4. **Monitor quote patterns** - which senior officers issue most statements

### For Data Quality
1. **Never activate pattern-inferred emails** - only use explicitly published contacts
2. **Cross-reference all office lines** against official PDRM directory
3. **Flag mobile-only IOs** - may indicate field investigators vs. desk officers
4. **Track extension changes** - may indicate office moves or restructuring

### For Coverage Analysis
1. **Outlet bias detection** - which outlets get most IO quotes?
2. **Regional coverage gaps** - which states/districts underrepresented?
3. **Case type patterns** - which outlets cover dadah vs. komersil vs. jenayah?
4. **Language analysis** - BM vs. English outlet differences
