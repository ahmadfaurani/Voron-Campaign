## Summary

## Key Outcome: Weststar–RTI Strategic Collaboration Agreement

The landmark signing establishes a formal partnership between **Weststar Defence Industries** (Malaysia) and **Rocket Technologies International** (Australia) in the aerospace and defence sectors. RTI operates the Helidon Energetix & Test Complex in Queensland — a specialist facility for propulsion and energetics testing.
