### 1A. 🆕 NEW — KPN Media Statement: PDRM Protecting Official Identity from Commercial Exploitation (July 15, 2026)
- **Title:** "KENYATAAN MEDIA KETUA POLIS NEGARA — PDRM Tegas Memelihara Integriti dan Identiti Rasmi Institusi Daripada Eksploitasi Bagi Tujuan Komersial"
- **URL (PDRM Portal):** https://www.rmp.gov.my/laman-utama (Berita Semasa #1)
- **URL (Kosmo):** https://www.kosmo.com.my/2026/07/15/pdrm-tegas-pelihara-integriti-identiti-rasmi-daripada-eksploitasi-komersial/
- **Date:** 15 July 2026, 10:59 AM MYT
- **Speaker:** Tan Sri Mohd. Khalid Ismail, Ketua Polis Negara (IGP)

**Key Facts:**
- PDRM views seriously any use of its **name, symbols/emblems, officer images, or any official identity** for promotion, marketing, advertising, or commercial activities.
- Presence of PDRM officers at any programme **does NOT constitute** endorsement, support, or recognition of any product/service/investment/commercial activity.
- Public advised not to assume a product/service has PDRM approval based solely on PDRM personnel involvement in a programme.

**Key Quote:**
> "Identiti rasmi PDRM merupakan aset institusi yang perlu dipelihara dan tidak boleh dieksploitasi bagi memperoleh keuntungan atau mewujudkan persepsi yang mengelirukan."
— Tan Sri Mohd. Khalid Ismail, KPN

**Legal Consequences:**
- Investigation and legal action against parties illegally using PDRM identity
- Under **Section 233, Communications and Multimedia Act 1998 (Act 588)**
- Subject to provable facts and elements of the offence

**Relevance:** Post-election institutional integrity directive. Not election-specific but indicates PDRM returning to routine governance priorities. The CMA Section 233 invocation is notable — same statute used for election-related online offences during the Johor campaign.
