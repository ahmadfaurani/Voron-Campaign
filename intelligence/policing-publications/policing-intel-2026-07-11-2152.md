## 2. ELECTION SECURITY — PRN JOHOR (CRITICAL)

### 2.1 KPN Press Conference — Polling Day (July 11, 2026, ~1:00 PM)
**Source**: Kosmo, Harian Metro, Berita Harian — all reporting same event
**Location**: After visiting polling at SMK Taman Molek, Johor Bahru

#### Key Facts:
- **Ketua Polis Negara (IGP): Tan Sri Mohd Khalid Ismail** held press conference
- **90 police reports** received during campaign period (up to eve of polling day)
- **25 investigation papers** opened involving various election-related offenses
- Cases described as "minor matters" not affecting public order
- Investigations ongoing to identify individuals involved before further action

#### Types of Offenses Recorded:
- Destroying, damaging, and burning political party flags (perbuatan khianat)
- Cases investigated under:
  - **Kanun Keseksaan (Penal Code)**
  - **Akta Komunikasi dan Multimedia (Communications and Multimedia Act)**
  - **Akta Kesalahan Pilihan Raya (Election Offenses Act)**

#### Key Quotes:
> "Setakat ini kita menerima 90 laporan polis sepanjang tempoh berkempen sehingga semalam dan telah membuka 25 kertas siasatan. Kebanyakannya membabitkan perkara-perkara kecil yang saya percaya tidak akan menimbulkan keresahan atau ketegangan dalam kalangan pengundi serta petugas pilihan raya."
> — Tan Sri Mohd Khalid Ismail, KPN

> "Adalah menjadi tanggungjawab dan amanah PDRM memastikan aspek keselamatan, ketenteraman awam serta kawalan lalu lintas berada pada tahap terbaik supaya lebih 2.7 juta pengundi dapat menunaikan tanggungjawab mereka dengan selamat."
