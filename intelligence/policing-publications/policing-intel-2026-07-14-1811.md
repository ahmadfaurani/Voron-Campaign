## 3. KDN/MOHA — Security Directives & Policy (🔴 BREAKING)

### 3A. 🔴 MAJOR: KDN Investigation — Alleged Israeli Involvement in Forest City Network School (TODAY, July 14, 2026)

**This is the lead security intelligence item for this cycle.**

- **Sources:** The Star, Malay Mail, Free Malaysia Today, Bernama, Channel News Asia — ALL July 14, 2026
- **URLs:**
  - The Star: https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2026/07/14/home-ministry-probes-alleged-presence-of-israelis-at-forest-city-school
  - Malay Mail: https://www.malaymail.com/news/malaysia/2026/07/14/johor-mb-calls-for-full-probe-into-alleged-israeli-participation-in-network-school-programme/227506
  - FMT: https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2026/07/14/johor-seeks-probe-into-network-school-over-alleged-israeli-links

**Key Facts:**
- Malaysia's **Home Ministry (KDN)** has launched a comprehensive investigation into the **Network School in Forest City, Johor**, over allegations that it hosted **Israeli nationals using second passports** (passports issued by other countries).
- **Agencies involved:** PDRM (Police), Immigration Department (JIM), working with "all relevant ministries and agencies."
- **Investigation scope:**
  - Nationalities of those involved
  - Status and validity of travel documents used
  - Whether activities were consistent with stated reasons for entry
  - Identities and nationalities of participants
  - Immigration passes used
  - Legal status of Network School's operations (premises licences, building use, land conditions, accommodation, business activities)

**Johor MB Statement:**
> "I wish to stress that the Johor government will not allow any party to use the state as a base to promote ideologies or movements that are contrary to the law, sovereignty and interests of Johor and Malaysia."
> "If investigations find any breach of the law, misuse of passes, violation of licensing or land conditions, or any element that could affect the state's interests and national security, the state government urges the authorities to take firm action without compromise."
— Datuk Onn Hafiz Ghazi, Johor Menteri Besar

**KDN Statement:**
> "The ministry views any matter involving national security, compliance with immigration laws and the misuse of Malaysia's immigration facilities [seriously]."
> "Should the investigation's findings reveal any violations of the law, misuse of immigration passes, provision of false information or any other offences that threaten national security and interests, firm action will be taken without compromise."

**About Network School:**
- Location: Forest City, Johor (man-made island development)
- Description: Residential technology community for entrepreneurs, remote workers, content creators
- Founder: **Balaji Srinivasan** — technology investor, former Coinbase Chief Technology Officer
- Concept: Co-living/co-learning community for tech founders

**National Security Context:**
- Malaysia has **NO diplomatic relations with Israel**
- Use of second passports allegedly allowed Israeli nationals to bypass potential entry restrictions
- This raises significant national security, immigration law compliance, and counterintelligence concerns
- Occurs in Johor — the same state where the PRN election just concluded 3 days prior

**ALERT TRIGGER:** This meets multiple alert criteria — KDN/MOHA emergency directive, national security investigation, PDRM/Immigration joint operation in Johor. **Recommend immediate escalation.**
