## 1. PDRM OFFICIAL — KPN STATEMENT (11 JULAI 2026, POSTED 13 JULAI)

**Source:** rmp.gov.my official news portal
**URL:** https://www.rmp.gov.my/news-detail/2026/07/13/prn-keselamatan-di-seluruh-johor-terkawal-dan-proses-undian-berjalan-lancar---kpn
**Title:** "PRN: KESELAMATAN DI SELURUH JOHOR TERKAWAL DAN PROSES UNDIAN BERJALAN LANCAR - KPN"

### Key Facts:
- **KPN (IGP):** Tan Sri Dato' Seri Hj. Mohd Khalid Hj. Ismail conducted field visit on polling day (11 July 2026)
- **Personnel deployed:** 14,000+ officers and personnel across Johor (including reinforcements from other contingents)
- **5,600 officers/personnel** brought in from contingents nationwide for election duty
- **Voter roll:** 2,727,926 registered voters; 69.57% turnout

### KPN Quotes:
> "Alhamdulillah, setakat ini keadaan keselamatan di seluruh Johor berada dalam keadaan terkawal dan proses pengundian berjalan lancar."
> "PDRM akan terus mempertingkat pemantauan dan penugasan sehingga keseluruhan proses pilihan raya selesai bagi memastikan keselamatan awam, ketenteraman serta kelancaran proses demokrasi terus terpelihara."

### Deployment Scope:
- Kawalan di pusat-pusat mengundi (polling station security)
- Rondaan pencegahan jenayah (crime prevention patrols)
- Pengurusan lalu lintas (traffic management)

- Pemantauan berterusan di lokasi strategik (continuous monitoring at strategic locations)

## 2. ELECTION SECURITY — CAMPAIGN PERIOD ENFORCEMENT

### Cumulative Statistics (27 June – 10 July 2026):

| Metric | Final Count (as of 11 July) |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Police reports received | **90** |
| Investigation papers opened | **25** |
| Campaign permit applications | 4,368 |
| Permits approved | 4,053 (~92.8%) |
| Political programmes monitored | 3,182+ |
| Candidates implicated | **0** (confirmed by IGP) |

### Growth of Investigation Papers Over Campaign:
- Jul 3: 8 IPs
- Jul 7: 18 IPs (56 reports)
- Jul 9: 22 IPs (73 reports)
- Jul 11: 25 IPs (90 reports)

### Nature of Offences:
- Most cases: Acts of mischief — removal, destruction, burning of political party flags
- Minor vandalism and damage to campaign materials
- NO major security incidents reported
- NO candidates or senior party leaders implicated in any reports

### Statutes Invoked for Investigations:
- Election Offences Act 1954 (Section 4A(1) — promoting ill will/hostility; Section 24B)
- Penal Code (Section 427 — mischief/damage; Section 500 — defamation)
- Communications and Multimedia Act 1998 (Section 233 — improper use of network facilities)

### Early Voting (Police Personnel):
- 12,706 police officers/personnel/spouses registered as early voters
- 95.42% turnout (11,511 voters)
- 643 postal votes cast
- Process completed smoothly

## 3. POLLING DAY SECURITY ASSESSMENT

- Polling conducted peacefully on 11 July 2026, 8am–6pm
- NO untoward incidents reported at polling stations
- IGP expressed satisfaction with overall process after visiting multiple voting centres
- Security situation across all 56 constituencies remained under control
- Handful of voter complaints received; complainants advised to lodge police reports for EC action
- IGP urged continued monitoring through completion of entire electoral process

---

## 4. ELECTION RESULTS (Context for Security Assessment)

- **BN landslide victory:** 48/56 seats (59.66% vote share), +8 from previous
- **PH:** 8/56 seats (32.64%), -4
- **PN:** 0/56 seats (5.45%), complete wipeout, -3
- **55 candidates** forfeited deposits (failed to secure 12.5% of votes) = RM275,000 to EC
- PN lost highest forfeitures: 21 of 33 candidates
- Turnout: 69.57% (1,897,892 total votes; 98.79% valid)
- 11 seats flipped; 9 to BN from PH/PN
- Result widely seen as BN consolidation; no post-results security incidents reported
