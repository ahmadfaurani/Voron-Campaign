## Executive Summary

**Total Media Universe:** 
- **Baseline:** 100–150 formal broadcast and mainstream news/media outlets
- **Expanded:** 150–250+ outlets including digital-native, niche, regional, community, and platform-based media

**Regulatory Framework:** MCMC Content Applications Service Provider (CASP) licensing covers:
- Satellite broadcasting
- Subscription broadcasting
- Terrestrial free-to-air TV
- Terrestrial radio broadcasting
- Internet content applications (separate category)

**Strategic Implication:** Political monitoring requires multi-tier approach covering broadcast, print, digital-native, regional, and language-specific media to capture full narrative landscape.

## Collection Priority Matrix

### P1: Critical (Monitor Real-Time)

- Bernama (wire service — sets official narrative)
- The Star (English mainstream — business/elite)
- Malaysiakini (independent — breaking news leader)
- NST (government perspective — policy signals)
- FMT (alternative — viral content detector)
- RTM TV1 (state broadcaster — official announcements)
- TV3 (commercial — mass Malay audience)
- Astro AWANI (24h news — continuous coverage)
- Sinar Harian (rural Malay — sentiment barometer)

### P2: High (Monitor 2-3x Daily)

- Berita Harian, Harian Metro (Malay mainstream)
- Malay Mail (English alternative)
- The Edge (business-politics nexus)
- Sin Chew Daily (Chinese community)
- China Press (Chinese business)
- Tamil Nesan (Indian community)
- Daily Express, Borneo Post (Sabah/Sarawak)
- Media Prima radio (Buletin updates)
- KiniTV, The Vibes, MalaysiaNow (digital-native)

### P3: Medium (Monitor Daily)

- Utusan Malaysia (UMNO perspective)
- Nanyang Siang Pau, Guang Ming (Chinese alternative)
- Malaysia Nanban (Tamil alternative)
- Regional outlets (TVS, Sabah TV)
- Business media (Focus Malaysia, Digital News Asia)
- Podcasts, YouTube channels
- Influencer analysts (Twitter, columns)

### P4: Low (Monitor Weekly or Event-Driven)

- Community newsletters
- NGO publications (Aliran, PEMANTAU)
- Grassroots social media
- Platform-native content (TikTok, Instagram political)
- State government publications

## Integration with DeerFlow Skills

### political-news-collection Skill

**Current Coverage:** 5 Tier 1 sources (Bernama, Star, Malaysiakini, NST, FMT)

**Expansion Plan:**
- Add Tier 2: Sinar Harian, BH, HM, Malay Mail, The Edge

- Regional: Daily Express, Borneo Post, TVS

### entity-extraction Skill

**Media Entity Types:**
- `MEDIA_OUTLET` — Organization subtype
- `JOURNALIST` — Person subtype (political reporters, editors)
- `NEWS_DESK` — Organizational unit (e.g., "Star Politics Desk")
- `PROGRAM` — Broadcast shows (Buletin Utama, 7th Edition)

### social-media-monitor Skill

**Broadcast Social Presence:**
- Monitor Twitter/X accounts of TV news desks
- Track Facebook pages of radio stations
- YouTube channels for video news content
- Instagram for visual political content

### daily-brief-generator Skill

**Media Coverage Section:**
- Which outlets covered top stories
- Narrative divergence across media clusters
- Broadcast vs. digital framing differences
- Language-specific narrative variations
