## Key Findings

1. **Lonpac Insurance achieves 7/7 full coverage** — The first insurance company in the database to reach complete 7-role coverage. Both new roles came from the LPI Capital (parent company) official management team page.

2. **Manulife Annual Report 2024** is a high-value source — provided CFO and CIO for both Manulife Insurance Berhad and Manulife Takaful Malaysia Berhad. However, the Takaful entity may not exist separately (management is shared with Manulife Insurance Berhad).

3. **CISO remains the hardest role to fill** — confirmed across all 15 institutions researched. CISO positions are consistently NOT publicly disclosed by Malaysian financial institutions, likely for security reasons. This is the primary bottleneck preventing institutions from reaching 7/7 coverage.

4. **The Org (theorg.com)** provided 3 roles for AmGeneral Insurance (Kurnia) at MEDIUM confidence, all marked "Unverified" on the source itself. These should be verified via LinkedIn or official sources.

5. **Image-based leadership pages** (Sun Life Malaysia) make automated extraction impossible — names/titles are baked into image files with no HTML text. Required LinkedIn cross-referencing.
