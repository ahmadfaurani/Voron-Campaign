## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Strategic Context
The **6 SWING seats** represent the highest-priority battleground constituencies for PRN Johor 2026. These seats are characterized by:
- **Competitive margins** (historical swings between coalitions)
- **Demographic volatility** (significant youth voter cohorts)
- **Turnout sensitivity** (2022 saw dramatic turnout drops vs 2018)
- **Multi-cornered fight potential** (3+ candidate scenarios likely)

### Aggregate Demographics (6 SWING Seats Combined)

| **Indian Voters** | 6.8% (swing factor) |

| **Avg Turnout 2022** | 55.5% (vs 82.3% in 2018) |
| **Avg Turnout 2018** | 82.3% |

### Key Intelligence Findings

1. **Youth Surge:** 18-29 age group is the LARGEST cohort in ALL 6 seats (23.5-27.5%)
2. **Turnout Collapse:** 2022 turnout dropped 20-30 percentage points vs 2018 across all seats
3. **Malay Majority:** All 6 seats have Malay majorities (55-61%), making Malay vote consolidation critical
4. **Chinese Decisive:** Chinese voters (29-35%) hold balance of power in close races
5. **Indian Swing:** Indian voters (7-13% in some seats) can tip marginal contests

- **Priority Cohorts:** Youth (18-29) + Chinese voters showed largest drop-off
