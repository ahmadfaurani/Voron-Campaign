## Strategic Analysis

### Malay-Majority Districts (>70% Malay)
**Total voters: 10,982 (43.6% of constituency)**
1. FELDA LAYANG-LAYANG - 99.1% Malay (875 voters) - *Classic UMNO stronghold*
2. KAMPONG CHOKRO - 90.3% Malay (4,059 voters) - *Largest Malay-majority district*
3. KAMPONG SAHARI - 83.4% Malay (4,206 voters) - *Second largest district*
4. RENGGAM - 83.1% Malay (832 voters)
5. LAYANG-LAYANG - 77.7% Malay (1,683 voters)

**Strategic implication:** These 5 districts form the **BN/UMNO base**. Need >60% margin here to offset Chinese-majority areas.

### Chinese-Majority Districts (>50% Chinese)
**Total voters: 9,891 (39.3% of constituency)**
1. BANDAR LAYANG-LAYANG SELATAN - 69.8% Chinese (3,296 voters) - *Largest Chinese district, PH/DAP stronghold*
2. BANDAR LAYANG-LAYANG UTARA - 58.9% Chinese (2,986 voters) - *Urban, PH-leaning*
3. BANDAR RENGGAM - 46.2% Chinese (3,609 voters) - *Mixed but Chinese-plurality*
4. KEBUN BAHRU - 42.4% Chinese (820 voters) - *Mixed, swing district*

**Strategic implication:** These 4 districts are **PH/PKR target areas**. High turnout essential.

### Mixed/Swing Districts
**Total voters: 4,308 (17.1% of constituency)**
1. SENDA - 31.4% Malay, 22.6% Chinese, 44.9% Indian (867 voters) - *Indian-plurality, highly competitive*
2. CHEMARA - 53.0% Malay, 50.3% Indian (1,450 voters) - *Malay-Indian mix*
3. SEMBRONG - 35.8% Malay, 54.0% Indian (137 voters) - *Small, Indian-majority*
4. LADANG SOUTHERN MALAY - 39.4% Malay, 43.7% Indian (142 voters) - *Small, Indian-plurality*

**Strategic implication:** **Kingmaker districts** - Indian vote share critical here. MIC or PH influence could swing results.

## Tactical Recommendations

### For PH/PKR Campaign:
1. **Maximize turnout** in BANDAR LAYANG-LAYANG SELATAN & UTARA (6,282 voters, 69.8% & 58.9% Chinese)
2. **Court Indian voters** in SENDA, CHEMARA, SEMBRONG (1,856 voters) - key swing bloc
3. **Damage limitation** in Malay heartlands - target 30-35% instead of trying to win
4. **Youth mobilization** - 23.8% aged 18-30, likely reformasi-leaning

### For BN/UMNO Campaign:
1. **Run up margins** in FELDA, KAMPONG CHOKRO, KAMPONG SAHARI (9,140 voters, 85%+ Malay)
2. **Defend police vote** - 652 police voters, traditional BN base
3. **Split Indian vote** - compete in SENDA, CHEMARA to prevent PH consolidation
4. **Senior voter outreach** - 23.6% aged 60+, likely conservative
