## Regulatory Framework

Bank Rakyat operates under a unique triple regulatory structure:

1. **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM)** - Under Development Financial Institutions Act (DAFIA 2002)
2. **Ministry of Entrepreneur Development and Cooperatives (MEDAC)** - Cooperative oversight
3. **Cooperatives Commission of Malaysia** - Cooperative compliance

This makes Bank Rakyat distinct from conventional commercial banks in Malaysia.
