## Corporate History

### 1970s-1980s: Foundation
- **1975:** Founded as Persian-Malaysian Development Bank by Hussain Najadi
- **1976:** Commenced operations (1 April)
- **1982:** Tan Sri Dato' Azman Hashim acquired 100% shareholding
- **1991:** AMMB Holdings Berhad incorporated; AMFB listed on KLSE

### 1990s-2000s: Expansion
- **1994:** Ventured into commercial banking (Arab-Malaysian Bank Berhad)
- **2000s:** ANZ Group became major shareholder (23.78%)

### 2010s-Present: 1MDB Scandal & Recovery
- **2015-2020:** Central to 1MDB scandal
- **2021:** RM 1.8 billion 1MDB settlement payment to Malaysian government
- **2019:** Voon Seng Chuan appointed Chairman replacing Azman Hashim
- **2023-Present:** Ongoing transformation under Jamie Ling's leadership
