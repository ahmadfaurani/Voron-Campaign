## Key Findings

### Best Results
- **RHB Bank**: 100% coverage (7/7) — Official Group Senior Management page listed 6 of 7 roles directly
- **Bank Islam**: 100% coverage (7/7) — Official leadership page + Bloomberg + LinkedIn for CRO/CIO

### Gaps Identified
- **GRC Role**: Not found at 5 of 7 institutions — Most Malaysian banks distribute GRC across CRO + Compliance + Legal roles rather than having a dedicated Head of GRC
- **CISO Role**: Not found at 2 of 7 institutions — CISO often sits below senior management tier and isn't publicly listed
- **Hong Leong Bank**: Only 1 HIGH confidence result — Bank doesn't publicly list senior management; relied on The Org (crowd-sourced)

### Notable Patterns
1. **Official leadership pages** remain the fastest, highest-confidence source (RHB, AmBank, Alliance, Bank Islam, UOB all had accessible management pages)
2. **GRC as a distinct role** is rare in Malaysian banking — typically merged into CRO or Legal/Governance roles
3. **CISO** is often a sub-management role not listed on public leadership pages
4. **Dual-role executives** are common: Jacob Abraham (Alliance CRO + Compliance), Nik Azmir Nik Anis (Bank Islam Compliance + GRC)
