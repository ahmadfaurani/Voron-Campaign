## Methodology Notes

### Collection Strategy
1. **Primary:** Direct URL extraction from official leadership pages (fastest, highest confidence)
2. **Secondary:** Web search for specific executive names and appointments
3. **Tertiary:** LinkedIn enrichment for supplementary data (MEDIUM confidence)

### Confidence Scoring
- **HIGH (90-100):** Official bank leadership pages, press releases, annual reports
- **MEDIUM (70-89):** LinkedIn profiles, third-party org charts (The Official Board, RocketReach)
- **LOW (<70):** Unverified news articles, outdated sources

### Role Mapping
Target 7 roles mapped to actual titles:
1. CEO → CEO, President, Group Chief Executive
2. CFO → Chief Financial Officer, Group CFO
3. CRO → Chief Risk Officer, Group CRO
4. CIO/CTO → Chief Information Officer, Chief Technology Officer, Head of IT
5. Compliance → Chief Compliance Officer, Head of Compliance, General Counsel
6. Audit → Chief Internal Auditor, Head of Internal Audit, Audit Committee Chair
7. GRC → Head of GRC, or combination of CRO + Compliance Officer
