## Key Observations

1. **Digital banks are highly transparent** — Boost Bank, Ryt Bank, and GX Bank all have detailed leadership pages with multiple C-suite executives listed
2. **TNG Digital's combined role** — Dunstan Gerald Maurice's "Chief Risk, Compliance & Governance Officer" title covers 3 of the 7 target roles in one position
3. **PayNet has limited public disclosure** — Only CEO, CFO, CTO, and CCO listed; no risk, compliance, audit, or security roles publicly disclosed
4. **Zurich Malaysia only lists CEOs** — No functional C-suite (CFO, CRO, CISO, etc.) on their leadership page
5. **CISO remains hardest to fill** (16.1%) — Only 2 new CISOs found this cycle (iPay88 + existing)
6. **Head of Internal Audit had zero new entries** — This role is rarely listed on corporate websites
7. **Fintech/digital bank segment improved significantly** — T6 coverage jumped from 13.3% to 37.1% due to Boost Bank, Ryt Bank, and TNG Digital additions
8. **Payment operators still need work** — PayNet at 2/7; DuitNow, FPX, JomPAY remain at 0/7 (PayNet sub-services)
