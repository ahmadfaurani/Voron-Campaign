## Methodology Notes

1. **JS-rendered pages:** Sun Life Malaysia management team page was resistant to all extraction methods (web_extract, Firecrawl scrape with waitFor, browser_navigate, browser_console). Raw HTML contained no member data. Only the financial statement PDF yielded leadership data.
2. **Firecrawl query format:** The "query" format with freeform mode proved highly effective for extracting structured data from JS-rendered leadership pages (Generali, Manulife corporate governance).
3. **PDF extraction:** web_extract successfully converted PDFs (Manulife Annual Report 2024, Citibank board document, SLMA Financial Statement) to structured markdown with leadership data.
4. **Website redesigns:** AmBank Group (ambankgroup.com) is mid-redesign with all leadership pages returning 404, blocking Kurnia/AmGeneral enrichment.
5. **Entity restructuring:** Manulife Takaful Malaysia Berhad may have been restructured — not listed as MHB subsidiary in FY2024 Annual Report.
