### Key Insight: CISO Role Rarity Confirmed

This run systematically verified that **CISO remains the rarest role** (32.0% fill rate, up from 30.2%). Of the 20 institutions at 6/7 coverage:
- **4** had a CISO-equivalent (CTO/CIO/IT Head who covers infosec) — added with MEDIUM confidence
- **12** had **no publicly listed CISO at all** — audit notes added
- **1** had a CIO match (ASNB → PNB Group CTO) — added with HIGH confidence
- **3** had other missing roles (Bank Rakyat IA, Phillip GRC, Tekun GRC) — all NOT FOUND

Malaysian DFIs (Agrobank, BPMB, EXIM Bank, SME Bank, Tabung Haji) consistently do not publish a CISO title. The function is either:
1. Embedded under Group CITO / Chief Digital & Technology Officer
2. Absorbed into Risk & Compliance department
3. Not publicly disclosed
