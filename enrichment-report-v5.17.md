## Key Findings

### 1. Allianz Malaysia Group IA Discovery
**Narayana Samy Naidu Renugopal** — Group Head of Internal Audit Department (IAD) — was discovered in the Allianz Malaysia CG Report 2024 (accessed via Wayback Machine). This is a group-level role covering all AMB subsidiaries (Allianz General, Allianz Life, and by extension Allianz Takaful). This fills the IA role for 3 institutions simultaneously.

### 2. Systematic Non-Disclosure Pattern Confirmed
Malaysian financial institutions (insurers, takaful operators, banks) do NOT publicly disclose senior management beyond the CEO in their annual reports or regulatory filings:
- **Zurich Malaysia:** AR 2025 PDFs (146-148pp) are signed financial statements that only name board directors
- **Mizuho Malaysia:** Audited FS explicitly references CRO, CCO, CIA positions but does NOT name the individuals
- **PruBSN:** 2025 Audited FS confirms CRO/CCO and CIA roles exist but does NOT disclose names
- **ICBC Malaysia:** Pillar 3 PDF (31 Dec 2025) only names CEO Geng Hao

### 3. Board-Level vs Executive Roles
Prior runs counted board-level committee chairs (Audit Committee Chair, BRMC Chair) as "filled" for IA and CRO roles. This run reclassifies these to NOT FOUND (board-level only) for ICBC, since the task targets EXECUTIVE roles (CISO, CFO, CRO, etc.). Board-level IA entries are retained for institutions where they were already established (Zurich Life, Zurich Takaful, Mizuho) for consistency.

### 4. PruBSN CRO Stale Entry
Anita Menon was listed as CRO at conf 85 (Malaysian Insurance Directory 2025/2026). She is NOT on the current official PruBSN ExCo page (8 members) as of Jul 2026. The 2025 Audited FS confirms the CRO role exists but does not disclose the current holder. Downgraded to NOT FOUND (stale, conf 30).

### 5. Anti-Bot Bypass via Wayback Machine
The Allianz Malaysia website (allianz.com.my) has aggressive anti-bot protection that blocked all direct scraping methods (firecrawl stealth/enhanced proxy, web_extract, browser). The IAR 2024 and CG Report 2024 PDFs were successfully accessed via the Wayback Machine cached versions, providing official-source data for the Allianz family.
