## Key Findings

1. **Insurance/Takaful leadership pages** typically list CEO, COO, CFO, and business-line heads (agency, claims, underwriting) but rarely publicly disclose CISO, internal audit, compliance, or GRC heads separately.

2. **Annual reports** are the richest source for senior management details — the MSIG Annual Report 2025 and QBE Annual Report 2024 both confirmed full senior management listings.

3. **Digital banks** (AEON Bank, KAF Digital, Ryt Bank) have minimal public leadership data beyond CEO appointments, as they are newly licensed and still building out their executive teams.

4. **Allianz Malaysia** website actively blocks automated extraction (antibot), making it difficult to access their corporate governance and annual report pages programmatically.

5. **Ryt Bank** (formerly SeaBank Malaysia) is a significant find — it's a JV between YTL and Sea Limited, with 4/7 roles already captured from their official website.
